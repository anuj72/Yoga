Welcome to Yoga / Meditation Wellness Retreat Center

We help people in maintaining a healthy state of mind and body through consultations and wellness tourism. We help people to transform lives for the better by restoring equilibrium with tangible and sustainable wellness benefits.

What we do

  1. Consultation

Here at 360 Wellness Solutions we have a dedicated team to consult and guide people about wellness, and also design their wellness retreat as per their requirement of wellness.

  1. Yoga & Meditation Retreat Center for Individuals

For individual potential wellness seekers, when consults us online with their requirements regarding Yoga, Meditation Retreats Centre, our doctors suggest few retreat that would include therapies and/or medicines.

Once confirmation is provided from your side. Our team comprising of doctors from different fields start working on it analyse the case history and decide which all therapies would best suite and accordingly the idea is suggested.

As soon as the seeker reaches India, our representative gets in touch with the seeker and briefs the seeker in detail about their retreat.

  1. Group Retreat

We also arrange group retreats, wherein there are some specific dedicated retreats planned. In this case group is received from airport and are taken to the venue in the Himalayas, where the retreat has been planned and there the group goes through theoretical and practical sessions of Yoga, Meditation, Therapy, Mudras, Homeopathy, Physiotherapy, Scientific Vastu, and Sattvic diet sessions; all at one place.
