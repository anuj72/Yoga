Physiotherapy Wellness Re-Treatment

Physiotherapy is a natural or physical medicine. It is performed to improve body’s functional status and can be used as preventive or in rehabilitation. It has different specialties. Hippocrates and Galen are supposed to have been worked as physiotherapist. Towards the end of 19th century modern physiotherapy was born.

Our well experienced physiotherapists have been working in preventive arena with the patients in order to improve their functional abilities, sleep quality, and to help them attain physical balance. Modified version of physiotherapy modalities along with Yoga helps immensely in bringing mind and body balance.
It is one of the most demanded therapies for the betterment of body and mind connection. Learn new methods of keeping yourself on track in terms of physical and mental wellness.
